Pancakes

Easy and quick recepie for your special sweet cravings

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Sugar
  • Leaveners - baking soda or baking power
  • Salt
  • Buttermilk
  • Milk
  • Eggs
  • Butter

How to make pancakes

  1. Mix the dry ingredients
    Combine:
    • Flour
    • Sugar
    • baking powder
    • Baking soda
    • Salt
    Then combine in seperate bowl:
    • milk
    • eggs
    • Buttermilk
    • melted butter
  2. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat. You can flick water across the surface and if it beads up and sizzles, it's ready.
  3. Combine both mixtures, then use wooden spoon or fork to mix them. The batter will be a little lumpy, which is exactly what we need.
  4. Scoop up aproximately 1/2 of a cup of a mixture, and batter it on preheated griddle. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, which should take 1-2 minutes.
    Flip the pancake and cook until browned on the other side. Repeat for the rest of the mixture
  5. All done! Enjoy your fresh pancakes!
